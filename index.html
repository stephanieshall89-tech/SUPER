Chapter 1: The Myth of the Strong Black Woman

Reflection Journal

Myth vs. Truth Exercise

Write down 3 beliefs you’ve been taught about being a “strong” woman. Next to each, write the truth that aligns with your emotional, mental, and physical health.
Example: Myth — “Strong women don’t cry.” Truth — “Crying is a natural and healthy release.”

Chapter 2: Boundaries Are Not Betrayal

“I can’t give to that without .”

“I appreciate you thinking of me, but .”
